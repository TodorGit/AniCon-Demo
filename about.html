Our story

We are a team of anime and manga enthusiasts. We have been a part of the Anime convention world for many years and due to the ongoing pandemic situation we felt frustrated that we cannot share our passion for all things anime and manga as we had done until now.Thus we came up with the idea of an online Anime convention. Anicon is the first ever entirely online Anime and Manga convention. Thanks to our previous experience in the field (and their passion for it) we managed to convince many of our previous colleagues that and online convention is not only possible, but can be a lot of fun as well! Join us for this exiting experience.
